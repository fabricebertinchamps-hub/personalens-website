Я

"What do my own messages say about me?"

The introspective lens. Линза Я reads only YOUR messages — what you write, how you write, when you write it — and turns the pattern into a portrait of yourself. It's the lens that surfaces what you do without noticing, and where you have room to grow.

Who this lens is for

Three kinds of reader find Линза Я first.

The journaller

You already write to think. Линза Я does for your messages what journaling does for your mornings — but with a stranger's eye for pattern.

The personality-test reader

The quiet inventory-taker

The Nine Modules

What's inside a Я reading.

Each module is its own section in your report — its own card, its own colour, its own page. You can read them in order, or skip to the one you want.

  1. Persona CardYour archetype with a one-line superpower and a one-line kryptonite — designed to be screenshotted.
  2. Big Five ReadoutOpenness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, Neuroticism — decoded from how you actually write.
  3. Identity SignalsRecurring themes in your language. The values you keep returning to, even when you're not trying.
  4. Emotional WorldHow you feel inside the conversation. Mood arcs, energy curves, the moments your tone shifts.
  5. SuperpowersWhat you do better than most people. The way you defuse, the way you persuade, the way you arrive.
  6. PatternsThe loops you fall into — quietly. The phrases you repeat, the conversations you tend to derail.
  7. Connection StyleHow you reach for other people. Your bid for closeness when you want it, your retreat when you don't.
  8. Growth RoadmapThree specific, doable suggestions for what to try next — not vague self-improvement, actual moves.
  9. Shareable CardA finished image of your Persona Card, sized for Instagram Stories or a group chat.

Useful to know

What Линза Я does — and doesn't.

What Линза Я does

  • Analyzes the messages you wrote in the chat
  • Surfaces consistent patterns across many messages
  • Gives you a Big Five readout from observed behaviour
  • Names archetypes in a tone designed to be shareable, not clinical
  • Suggests three concrete things to try next

What Линза Я doesn't

  • It does not diagnose mental health conditions
  • It does not analyze the other person — that's Романтика / Работа
  • It does not predict your future behaviour or compatibility
  • It does not pretend a single chat captures your whole identity
  • It does not replace therapy, journaling, or talking to people who love you
