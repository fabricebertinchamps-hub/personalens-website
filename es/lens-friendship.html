Amistad

"What kind of friendship is this, actually?"

Lente Amistad analyses two things: the other person (your friend) and the friendship dynamic between you two. It finds the archetype, the communication DNA, the give-and-take rhythm, and the moments where the friendship is being quietly tested — or quietly rewarded.

Try Lente Amistad free

Who this lens is for

Three reasons people run it.

The "are we drifting?" reader

You haven't fought. You just notice the chat is quieter. Lente Amistad finds the words for what changed.

The "we're hilarious together" reader

You want a love letter to the friendship. The Communication DNA module is built for screenshots.

The "am I doing too much?" reader

Give-and-Take tells you who's holding the rope right now — and whether the imbalance is recent or always.

The Modules

What's inside a Amistad reading.

  1. Amistad ArchetypeOne named pair for both of you — "The Ride-or-Die", "The Wise Counsel", "The Anchor & The Spark", "The Old-Friend Cadence", and others.
  2. Communication DNAHow you two actually talk. Banter rhythm, callback rate, the in-jokes still working.
  3. Amistad PulseIs it growing, steady, or quietly cooling? With the specific moments behind the verdict.
  4. Give & TakeReciprocity score. Who chases the plans, who holds the emotional load, who shows up first.
  5. Emotional DepthHow much real talk lives in the chat — and how much it's swapped between you.
  6. Amistad PatternsThe loops you fall into. Sometimes endearing, sometimes a tell.
  7. TrajectoryWhere this friendship is heading at its current trajectory — and what would change it.
  8. Green FlagsLoyalty signals. Trust under pressure. The receipts of why this friendship is real.
  9. Watch ZonesThe fading inside joke, the one-sided effort, the slow ghost. Named gently.
  10. Shareable CardA finished image of your Amistad Archetype — designed to be sent to that exact friend.

Useful to know

What Lente Amistad does — and doesn't.

What Lente Amistad does

  • Reads both friends as equally interesting subjects
  • Names the dynamic — not just the people
  • Surfaces the quiet drift before you'd have noticed it
  • Celebrates the in-jokes that are still working
  • Gives you a card the friend will actually want screenshotted

What Lente Amistad doesn't

  • It does not decide who the better friend is
  • It does not give one of you a higher score than the other
  • It does not declare a friendship "over" — only describes the trajectory
  • It does not work well on chats shorter than ~50 messages
  • It is not the right lens for romantic ambiguity (use Romántica)
